Lasagna

Well cooked lasagna!

This recipe consists of minced beef,tomato and melted cheese!

It takes a little work, but tastes so satisfying!

Ingredients

  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps to take!

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
